# 🧠 Structured Prompts — KateyTea Copywriting Project

> These are the exact prompts used to generate professional website copy for KateyTea café in Boksburg using Claude AI.

---

## 📋 Master Prompt Template

This is the core prompt structure used. It can be reused for any local business by changing the business details.

```
You are an expert website copywriter specialising in local businesses. 
Your job is to write PUBLISH-READY website copy — not drafts, not suggestions, 
but actual final copy a business can paste onto their website today.

BUSINESS BRIEF:
- Business Name: KateyTea
- Type: Café / Coffee Shop
- Location: Boksburg, Gauteng
- Unique Selling Point: Hand-selected single-origin Ethiopian beans, slow-brewed every cup
- Target Customer: Locals, young professionals, students, remote workers
- Key Services: Espresso drinks, filter coffee, specialty teas, fresh-baked muffins, 
  toasted sandwiches, breakfast platters, co-working space, catering
- Brand Tone: Warm and Welcoming

COPYWRITING RULES YOU MUST FOLLOW:
• Lead with benefits, not features
• Use "you" language — speak directly to the customer
• Be specific — avoid vague words like "quality," "passion," "best"
• Every sentence must earn its place — no filler
• CTAs should be action-oriented and specific (not just "Contact Us")
• Tone must feel human, not corporate or AI-generated
• Write for Boksburg locals — feel local and grounded

GENERATE THE FOLLOWING SECTIONS:
1. HERO SECTION: A powerful H1 headline (max 10 words) + a subheadline (1-2 sentences) 
   that instantly communicates the core value and hooks the visitor.

2. VALUE PROPOSITION: A compelling 3-4 sentence paragraph that clearly answers: 
   Why choose us? What makes us different? Why does it matter to the customer? 
   Avoid clichés like "passionate" or "high quality".

3. SERVICE DESCRIPTIONS: For each service listed, write a 2-3 sentence description 
   that names the benefit first, then the feature. Make each one feel specific and desirable.

4. CALL-TO-ACTION SECTION: A persuasive CTA block with a punchy heading, 
   1 supporting sentence, and a clear action button label. 
   Make the action feel low-risk and high-reward.

5. ABOUT US BLURB: A 3-4 sentence human story-driven paragraph that builds 
   trust and connection without sounding corporate.

6. SEO META DESCRIPTION: A 150-160 character meta description with natural 
   keyword integration for a local search.

Format each section with a clear label like: === HERO SECTION === 
followed by the copy. Make the copy feel completely natural and ready to use. 
Do NOT include any meta-commentary, explanations, or alternatives — just the final copy.
```

## 🎯 Individual Section Prompts

### Prompt 1 — Hero Section

```
Write a homepage hero section for KateyTea, a café in Boksburg that serves 
hand-selected single-origin Ethiopian coffee, slow-brewed every cup.

Requirements:
- H1 headline: maximum 10 words, must mention Ethiopian coffee or Boksburg
- Subheadline: 1-2 sentences that explain the benefit to the customer
- Tone: Warm, inviting, specific — not generic
- Do NOT use words like: "passionate", "quality", "best", "dedicated"
- Speak directly to the customer using "you" and "your"
```

---

### Prompt 2 — Value Proposition

```
Write a value proposition paragraph for KateyTea café in Boksburg.

Key facts to include:
- They use hand-selected Ethiopian single-origin beans
- They slow-brew every cup (not rushed)
- The coffee has natural complexity — floral hints, berry undertones
- Customers come to work, meet friends, or just take a break

Rules:
- 3-4 sentences only
- Lead with what makes them different from other cafés
- End with why it matters to the customer
- No corporate language — sound human and local
```

---

### Prompt 3 — Service Descriptions

```
Write short service descriptions for KateyTea café in Boksburg.

Services to describe:
1. Espresso Drinks
2. Filter Coffee
3. Specialty Teas
4. Fresh-Baked Muffins
5. Toasted Sandwiches
6. Breakfast Platters
7. Co-Working Space
8. Catering

Rules for each description:
- 2-3 sentences maximum
- Start with the BENEFIT to the customer, not the feature
- Be specific — mention Ethiopian beans where relevant
- Sound like a real person wrote it, not a menu template
- Tone: warm, appetising, honest
```

---

### Prompt 4 — Call-to-Action

```
Write a call-to-action section for KateyTea café in Boksburg.

Business details:
- Phone: 011 047 4849
- Location: Boksburg, Gauteng

Requirements:
- Punchy heading that creates desire (not just "Visit Us")
- 1 supporting sentence that removes hesitation
- A clear button label that tells the customer exactly what to do
- Mention Boksburg so it feels local
- Make it feel urgent but not pushy
- Tone: welcoming and confident
```

---

### Prompt 5 — About Us

```
Write an About Us section for KateyTea café in Boksburg.

Key facts:
- It is a local café, not a chain or franchise
- They serve Ethiopian coffee
- They care about their community in Boksburg
- They want customers to feel welcome and stay as long as they like

Requirements:
- 3-4 sentences
- Sound human and warm — like the owner is speaking
- Build trust without bragging
- Do NOT use: "passionate", "dedicated", "committed", "strive"
- End with an open invitation to visit
```

---

### Prompt 6 — SEO Meta Description

```
Write an SEO meta description for KateyTea café in Boksburg.

Requirements:
- Between 150-160 characters exactly
- Include: KateyTea, Boksburg, Ethiopian coffee
- Mention at least one service (coffee, muffins, breakfast)
- End with a call to action
- Sound natural — not keyword-stuffed
```

## 🔁 How to Reuse These Prompts for Another Business

Simply replace the following details:

| Replace This | With Your Business Info |
|---|---|
| KateyTea | Your business name |
| Café / Coffee Shop | Your business type |
| Boksburg | Your location |
| Ethiopian beans, slow-brewed | Your unique selling point |
| Espresso, muffins, etc. | Your services |
| Warm and Welcoming | Your brand tone |

The prompt structure stays exactly the same. The AI will generate completely fresh, specific copy every time.
